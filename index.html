About The Event

Join Us for an Unforgettable Experience

Carnivalesque 4.0 is more than just an event – it’s a vibrant celebration of literature, imagination, and the performing arts, proudly hosted by the Amity Institute of English Studies and Research, Amity University, Patna. Across two immersive days, participants will have the opportunity to:

  • Enter a world where words come alive and stories take flight
  • Participate in events like Echoes of Lingo, Quizzards, Con-Closet, and Battle of Books
  • Express creativity through visual storytelling in Drawmics
  • Showcase your talent and energy in Dance and Frolics
  • Engage with passionate readers, writers, and performers from diverse backgrounds

    • Whether you're a passionate page-turner, a fearless debater, a vibrant performer, or a master of words—Carnivalesque 4.0 is your stage. Let’s celebrate the boundless magic of language together!
