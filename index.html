About The Event

Join Us for an Unforgettable Experience

LITERARIA 2025 is more than just an event – it’s a celebration of language, literature, and the limitless power of expression. Over three enriching days, participants will have the opportunity to:

  • Engage with celebrated authors, poets, and scholars
  • Participate in creative writing, debating, and dramatic arts competitions
  • Network with literature enthusiasts, educators, and cultural thinkers
  • Explore evolving trends in literature, media, and communication
